Description:

Gov Activity Dashboard is a dashboard that allows the user to interactively get two kinds of information:

  • WHO is doing censorship. That is, how active are governments in performing some kind of censorship on the Internet
  • the EFFECTS of censorship on the people. That is, how people "react" to censorship. It helps to answer this question: "is people from a specific country" aware of being censored"?
